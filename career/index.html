ENGLISH TEACHER RECRUITMENT

Love working with young students, especially with kids? Speak excellent English? Looking for a fulfilling, independent career? If you answered yes to these questions, becoming an English teacher at Future English Center (FEC) may be the best career choice you’ll ever make

WHY WORK AT FEC?

Becoming part of FEC teaching staff, you will have the opportunity to Earn a competitive salary or hourly rate of pay Get ongoing professional development & support via workshops, mentoring, classroom observations, etc. Have chances to work with expatriate teachers Work in a friendly, professional environment

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

To be eligible for a teaching position at FEC, applicants must have:

For Vietnamese English teachers:

We’re looking for people with:

  • Excellent command of English, both spoken and written.
  • Prior experience teaching English for children.
  • An academic background.
  • Weekday evening and weekend availability.

For Expatriate English Teachers:

We’re looking for people with:

  • BA degree in any discipline and a teaching certificate (CELTA, TESOL, TEFL, TKT, etc.)
  • Weekday evening and weekend availability

JOB DESCRIPTION

  • Preparing and delivering fully organized lessons that are provided
  • Classroom management
  • Marking and providing appropriate feedback to quizzes, writing assignments, etc.
  • Attending and contributing to training sessions
  • Basic administration: taking attendance, inputting grades, giving monthly feedback on student progress, etc.

HOW TO APPLY?

Application documents must be in English and include the following:

  • a cover letter
  • an updated resume
  • a copy of ID card/ passport
  • copies of degrees & teaching certificates
